ABOUT

I’m Atharv Maske, a Frontend Developer and UI/UX Designer currently pursuing Computer Engineering. I’m passionate about designing clean interfaces, building responsive websites, and creating digital experiences that are simple, modern, and meaningful.

My journey in technology has helped me explore both design and development. From working on academic projects to internship-based real-world tasks, I have gained hands-on experience in UI planning, frontend layouts, API understanding, project documentation, and product-based thinking.

I enjoy transforming ideas into polished digital products by combining design thinking with frontend development. My focus is not only on making interfaces look good, but also on making them easy to use, responsive, and practical for real users.

I work with tools and technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Tailwind CSS, Figma, Git, GitHub, VS Code, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and Postman. I am continuously improving my skills in modern web development, UI/UX design, responsive design, and software development practices.

My goal is to grow as a skilled frontend developer and UI/UX designer who can contribute to meaningful projects, solve real problems, and build digital products that deliver a smooth and professional user experience.
